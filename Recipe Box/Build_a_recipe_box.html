Strawberry Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 1 bowl of Strawberries
  • 250ml milk
  • 3 ice cubes

Instuctions:

  1. Wash all the strawberries and add to a blender.
  2. Add milk and ice cubes to the blender.
  3. Turn on the blender for 30secs.
  4. Pour it in a glass and put some Vennilla ice cream on top.
  5. Drink!

Makes 4 Servings!!!




Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instuctions:

  1. Place sweetened condensed milk in the fridge to keep cold.
  2. Using a hand or stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment, whip the cold cream on medium/high speed until soft peaks form.
  3. Turn off the machine and pour the cold condensed milk into the whipped cream.
  4. Turn the speed up to high and whisk until the mixture is thick and stiff peaks form. Turn off the machine and stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Transfer your ice cream mixture to a container.
  6. Refregerate minimum 6 hours before serving.

Makes 3 Servings!!!




Besan Ladoo

Ingredients:

  • 1 cups chickpea flour (besan)
  • 1 cup clarified butter (ghee)
  • 1 cup white sugar, or to taste
  • 2 teaspoons finely chopped pistachios and cashews

Instructions:

  1. Stir the chickpea flour and clarified butter together in a saucepan over medium-low heat until the mixture smells toasty, about 10 minutes.
  2. Set aside until cool enough to handle.
  3. Stir in the sugar, pistachios, and cashews until evenly mixed.
  4. Form the mixture into small balls.

Makes 12 Servings!!!
